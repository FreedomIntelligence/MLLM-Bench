MLLMBench

A Complicated and Comprehensive Multi-modal Large Language Models Benchmark
(2023)

 

About MLLMBench

MLLMBench is a complicated and comprehensive benchmarking suite inspired by the multifaceted nature of the vicuna and the revised Bloom's Taxonomy. MLLM-Bench encompasses 420 complicated image-instruction pairs across six levels of capabilities: Perception, Understanding, Applying, Analyzing, Evaluating, and Creation, testing up to 42 capabilities of MLLMs. It delves into detailed, real-world scenarios designed to probe the models’ abilities in nuanced and complex contexts. You can view and download our dataset on GitHub.

Sample

Instruction: Map out the complex interdependencies between species in this food web and identify any potential cascade effects if one species were to become extinct.
Capability: Natural Relation Understanding
Level: Analyzing

Instruction: Describe the quickest route to the nearest emergency exit from the "You Are Here" point.
Capability: Document Understanding
Level: Understanding


Taxonomy: 6 capability levels and 42 capabilities

Hierarchical Capability Level:

  • Perception: Have the basic capability to recognize and retrieve information from multi-modal inputs. Include 7 capabilities.
  • Understanding: Can process the information gathered during perception to construct meaning. Include 10 capabilities.
  • Applying: Apply the information and knowledge to new but similar situations. Include 7 capabilities.
  • Analyzing: Have the ability to break information into parts to explore patterns and relationships. Include 12 capabilities.
  • Evaluation: Make judgments based on criteria and standards. Include 4 capabilities.
  • Creation: Synthesize information from both visual and textual data to generate new conten. Include 2 capabilities.
